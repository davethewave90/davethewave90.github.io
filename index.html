Local Company Highlights:


Family Pet Hospital:
Open every weekday 8am - 6pm. Closed on weekends. Over 250 reviews on Google with an average rating of 4.5 stars. Headed by Dr. Grant Madsen, Family Pet Hospital offers "a full-service animal hospital whose mission is to provide the highest standard of veterinary care for pets..." They feature an easy-to-use website, a friendly staff of experts, and of course professional veterinary care. Family Pet Hospital is located on Main street here in Mapleton.
Maple Mountain Pharmacy:
Open Monday through Saturday 9am - 7pm. Closed on Sundays. Over 30 reviews on Google with an average rating of 4.1 stars. Staffed by Dr. Seth LeFevre and his pharmacy technicians. They boast a comprehensive website that allows appoints to be scheduled easily. Appointments can also be made over the phone with one of their friendly, competent staff members. Maple Mountain Pharmacy is also located here in Mapleton directly on Main street.
Sorenson Auto:
Open Tuesday - Friday 8am - 6pm. Closed Saturday - Monday. Over 80 reviews on Google with an average rating of 4.9 stars. They feature a web page through Facebook rather than host their own site. The online page does not offer appointments, but it does highlight their location and the phone number to reach them. Staffed by several expert mechanics they offer a wide array of auto repair services by appointments and drop-offs. One of the friendliest and most honest auto shops located right here in Mapleton just off the corner of Main street and 1600 N.

Upcoming events:


Fun Run: May 27th
Come join us for a fun and exciting run just for fun. This will be through the streets and parks of our gorgeous Mapleton. It is a 5K event so be sure to bring a friend and plenty of water!
Pioneer Day: July 24th
Pioneer Day! Saturday July 24th. Come one come all. Together let us celebrate with our friends and loved ones the pilgramige and the sacrifices made for us by our pioneer ancestors. This year we gather at Mapleton North Park. There are grills and shaded seating provided. These though are first-come first-serve. There is plenty of open park space available however to bring your own grill and seating. Please be respectful of others, wear a mask when not eating and practice social distancing when possible. And of course, a spectacular fireworks show at 9pm.

About Us:


City of Mapleton, Utah Chamber of Commerce. Our mission is to promote the businesses of Mapleton, Utah. For our businesses we seek to understand and support their needs and wants. For customes we strive to inform and supply any and all questions and concerns about their city's businesses. Our goal is nothing less than perfection when it comes to serving our great city, her businesses and most importantly, her people.
